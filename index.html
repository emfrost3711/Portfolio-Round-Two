About Me

Virginian by birth, a decade ago I uprooted from the South and pedaled my bike across the country. Ultimately circling back to the Mile High, I've called Colorado my home ever since. My deep-rooted curiosity and love of cultivating community have taken me from higher education to nonprofits to consulting and now into tech.

I love exploring possibilities, from front-end styling and functionality to conjuring seamless back-end code to employing agile workflow systems that honor the unique creative gifts of individuals and our collective genius, all with an aim to improve our daily lives.

Let's explore what's possible together!
